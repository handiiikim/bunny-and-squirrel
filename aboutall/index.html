Almost every Korean use this engine. Because of this, The government and huge companies started to manipulate this engine for their own sakes. For covering what they've done wrong, They replace the authentic trends and headlines to very absurd and unbelievable topics. Against this, Quite many Korean started to use SNS for sharing what the authority tries to hide.
